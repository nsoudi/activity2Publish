-Shows That I Am Currently Binging-



One show that i havent been able to stop watching as of late is a show called "Castlevania"




Castlevania is a Netflix original animation that follows and builds upon the lore of Dracula the worlds most famous and powerful vampire. The thing about the show that that has kept my interest for four seasons so far is that the plot of the show doesn't revolve around dracula. It focuses on exploring the relationship between morality and humanity through amazing story telling and animation.



Another show that I have been binge watching is "Euphoria"



Euphoria is an HBO original drama filled show that explores the complicated lives of young adults maneuvering their way through the world while struggling with real world issues.
